About Lumora

Lumora is a unique and inspiring brand that specializes in premium candles and luxurious dry oils. Built on passion and creativity, Lumora stands out for its dedication to quality, elegance, and attention to detail in every product. What makes Lumora truly special is the team behind it—only eight young ladies who carefully design, craft, and perfect each item. Their work reflects not just skill, but also a deep love for what they create. Every candle is thoughtfully made to provide a calming atmosphere, while the dry oils are designed to nourish the skin with a light, silky finish. Lumora combines simplicity with sophistication, offering products that bring comfort, beauty, and a touch of luxury to everyday life. It’s more than just a brand—it’s a story of ambition, teamwork, and creativity turned into something meaningful.
